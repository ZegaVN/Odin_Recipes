Steak Burrito


Description


For the meat-lovers and the burrito lovers, this is a good an really tasty recipe.

WIhout a doubt you will love the taste of this steak burrito, for those days that

you want something in a tortilla but not specifically a taco, just that something!


Ingredients


  • 1 (10-inch) flour tortilla
  • 3/4 cup cubed, cooked ribeye steak, warmed
  • 1/4 cup Mexican rice, heated
  • 1/4 cup pinto beans, heated
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

Steps


  1. Fold a paper towel around tortilla
  2. Microwave on High until pliable, 5 to 10 seconds.
  3. Lay tortilla flat on a plate
  4. Layer steak, rice, beans, cheese, onion, and cilantro in a line across the middle of the tortilla
  5. Fold opposing edges of the tortilla to overlap the filling
  6. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling and tuck tortilla to form a burrito
