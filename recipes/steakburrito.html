Steak Burrito


Description


For the meat-lovers and the burrito lovers, this is a good an really tasty recipe. WIhout a doubt you will love the taste of this steak burrito, for those days that you want something in a tortilla but not specifically a taco, just that something!


Ingredients


    1 (10-inch) flour tortilla 3/4 cup cubed, cooked ribeye steak, warmed 1/4 cup Mexican rice, heated 1/4 cup pinto beans, heated 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese 1 tablespoon diced onion 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
